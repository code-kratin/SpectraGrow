About

Plants are essential for life on Earth. They provide food, oxygen, medicine, resources for construction, and habitat for animals. Plants also help control the Earth's climate by absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen through photosynthesis. They help prevent soil erosion and enhance water availability. Furthermore, they provide us with a variety of materials, including timber, natural dyes and oils. Their aesthetic value is also invaluable. In short, plants help maintain a healthy and balanced environment on Earth.


Crop recommendation systems using machine learning are becoming increasingly popular. These systems use data such as temperature, rainfall, pH, and humidity for specific crops and apply different ML techniques to recommend crops with high accuracy and efficiency 12. These systems can be supportive for farmers to be more versatile.


There are many benefits to using crop recommendation systems. By analyzing data such as temperature, rainfall, pH, and humidity for specific crops, these systems can provide farmers with accurate and efficient recommendations for which crops to grow. This can help farmers to be more versatile and make better decisions about which crops to grow in order to maximize their yields and profits. Additionally, these systems can help to reduce the risk of crop failure by providing farmers with information about which crops are best suited to their specific growing conditions.
